Our Story

At RIA, we believe that home is a sanctuary, and our products are designed to enhance your living experience. That's why we're on a mission to create unique design-doven home
decor and lifestyle products that not only look good but also feel good. Our goal is to help you transform your space into a true reflection of your personality, style, and values.

Facing a lack of options, they set out to create a brand that offers unique, contemporary, and premium quality products for all areas of the house. RIA was hence created with
a mission to "Make Home Special"

Our product line is constantly evolving to keep up with the latest global trends, colors and styles, ensuring that we meet the expectations of modern consumers. Our customers are at the
heart of everything we do, and that's why we're constantly striving to innovate and improve. We have launched our first offline store in San Francisco so that customers can experience our
products firsthand. With more than 7000 products curated across 8 categories we have everything our patrons need to create a space that they truly love.





Sustainable Nest

Products

Inspiring creations & lifestyles, Nestasia shapes products inspired by sustainable
concepts. Aiming to minimizing our impact on the environment, we focus on artisanal
goods crafted from natural & renewable materials. We have carefully created a
selection of eco-friendly and sustainable products that support sustainable practices.

We believe that small changes can make a big impact, and that's why we are
committed to offering sustainable home decor & lifestyle products that make it easy
for you to make a positive difference in your home and the world around us

Packaging

As part of our efforts to minimize waste, we not only use scraps of fabrics left over
from our in-house production but also source it from neighbouring industries to create
unique and eco-friendly package fillers. These scraps are carefully collected and
repurposed, ensuring that every inch of fabric is utilized and nothing goes to waste.

By adopting these zero-waste packaging methods, we are able to significantly reduce
our environmental footprint and minimize the amount of waste that ends up in landfills.
